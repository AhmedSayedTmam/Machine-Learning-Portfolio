Loan Data from Prosper Exploration¶

by Ahmed Sayed¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I want to look deeper at factors that affect the the current status of the loan: Cancelled, Chargedoff, Completed, Current, Defaulted, FinalPaymentInProgress, PastDue.

Dataset Overview¶

The Dataset contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan. Variables are integers, floats and strings, including LoanStatus, LoanOriginalAmount, BorrowerAPR, StatedMonthlyIncome, Term, ProsperRating (Alpha), EmploymentStatus and many others.

Interest Rate and Loan Status¶

The highest Borrowers Rate are asscociated with loans that are charged-off and with loans that have past due payments

Monthly Loan Payment and Loan Status¶

The loans with current status have in average a higher Monthly Loan Payment

Borrower Rate and Loan Status in relation to the ProsperScore¶

The higher the Prosper Score, the lower the interest rate, so borrower with a low Prosper Score get a higher interest rate and the higher Prosper Score when a loan is completed.
